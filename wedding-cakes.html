✦ Good to Know ✦

Wedding Cake Questions

How far in advance should I book my wedding cake?

Wedding and event packages need at least 2–4 weeks of lead time — but dates are limited, so reach out as soon as you have your venue and date locked in. Kelsey will confirm availability for your day.

How much do wedding cakes cost?

Custom multi-tier wedding and event cakes start at $120. Build-Your-Own Cake Bar wedding packages range from $1,100 to $1,950. Kelsey confirms a final quote based on your size, design, and flavor choices.

Is a deposit required to book?

A 50% deposit is due at booking for all wedding and event packages, and may be required for large custom cake orders.

Do you deliver to wedding venues?

Yes — Sweet Confections delivers to venues across the Berkshires, Western Massachusetts, Eastern New York, Southern Vermont, and Northwest Connecticut, roughly a 1–2 hour radius from Pittsfield, MA. Delivery fees vary by distance.

Do you offer gluten-free wedding cake flavors?

Yes! Gluten-free cake flavors are available upon request for an additional charge, including Chocolate, Vanilla, Lemon Raspberry, Lemon Blueberry, Cookies and Cream, Red Velvet, Carrot Cake, Pumpkin, Chocolate Peanut Butter, and Strawberry Crunch.

What is the Build-Your-Own Cake Bar?

It's like a frozen yogurt bar — but for cake! Your guests choose their cake flavor, frosting, drizzle, and toppings to build a custom slice. Wedding packages include a beautifully styled table with fresh greens and florals, and range from $1,100 to $1,950.
