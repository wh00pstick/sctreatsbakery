Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

What we collect

When you place an order or send an inquiry through this website, we collect only what's needed to fulfill your order:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Order details you provide (flavors, design, pickup date, dietary needs, event info)
  • How you heard about us (optional)

We do not collect payment card information on this website. Any payment is arranged separately at pickup, delivery, or via a secure third-party processor (such as Square).

How we use it

Your information is used only to:

  • Confirm your order details and pricing with you
  • Coordinate pickup, delivery, or event service
  • Occasionally follow up about your experience

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Limited information may be shared with:

  • Payment processors (e.g. Square) if you choose to pay online
  • Delivery partners, if applicable to your order
  • Anyone we are legally required to disclose to

Cookies & tracking

This website does not currently use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. Your browser may store basic preferences locally (e.g. cart items) to make your visit smoother — this data stays on your device.

Your choices

You can request to see, correct, or delete any personal information we have about you at any time by emailing sctreatsshop@gmail.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email sctreatsshop@gmail.com.
