✦ Meet Kelsey ✦

Baked with love,
every single time.

"Bringing the taste of joy to every home!"

Hi, I'm Kelsey — the baker, the dreamer, and the one up at 4am making sure your cinnamon rolls are perfectly glazed. Sweet Confections started as a passion and has grown into something I pour my whole heart into every single day.

Based right here in Pittsfield, MA, I bake everything from scratch in my fully licensed and insured home kitchen — and I happily serve the whole Berkshires region and surrounding areas (Western MA, Eastern NY, Southern VT and Northwest CT, roughly a 1–2 hour drive). No mixes, no shortcuts — just real ingredients, real love, and a whole lot of butter.

✦ The Story ✦

From Passion
to Purpose

Baking has always been my happy place. There's something magical about turning simple ingredients — flour, butter, eggs, sugar — into something that makes people smile. I started Sweet Confections because I wanted to share that magic with my community.

What began with sharing treats with family and friends quickly grew into a real business, with orders for birthdays, baby showers, holidays, and everything in between. Every custom cake tells someone's story. Every batch of cookies is made to bring a moment of joy.

With over 1,600 followers on Facebook, a growing Instagram community, and a 100% five-star review record, Sweet Confections has become a beloved part of the Berkshires food scene.

"My dream is to have a food truck so I can bring Sweet Confections to weddings, festivals, and events all across the Berkshires. Every order you place gets me one step closer — thank you for being part of this journey." — Kelsey
