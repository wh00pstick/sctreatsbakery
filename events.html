✦ Good to Know ✦

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Build-Your-Own Cake Bar?

It's like a frozen yogurt bar — but for cake! Guests choose their cake flavor, frosting, and toppings to build a custom slice. Perfect for weddings, birthdays, showers, and corporate events. Available in 2 event packages ($150–$250) and 3 wedding packages ($1,100–$1,950).

Do you deliver outside Pittsfield, MA?

Yes! Sweet Confections delivers across the Berkshires, Western Massachusetts, Eastern New York, Southern Vermont, and Northwest Connecticut — roughly a 1–2 hour radius from Pittsfield, MA. Delivery fees vary by distance.

How far in advance should I order?

Cookies, brownies, pastries, and cupcakes need 24–48 hours notice. Custom cakes need at least 1 week. Wedding, event, and Cake Bar packages need 2–4 weeks. Rush orders are considered case-by-case.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes! Gluten-free cake flavors are available upon request for an additional charge. Available GF flavors include Chocolate, Vanilla, Lemon Raspberry, Lemon Blueberry, Cookies and Cream, Red Velvet, Carrot Cake, Pumpkin, Chocolate Peanut Butter, and Strawberry Crunch.

Is a deposit required?

A 50% deposit is required at booking for all event and wedding packages, and may be required for large custom cake orders. Standard same-week orders typically don't require a deposit.

Is Sweet Confections by Kelsey licensed?

Yes — Sweet Confections by Kelsey is a fully licensed and insured home bakery based in Pittsfield, Massachusetts.
